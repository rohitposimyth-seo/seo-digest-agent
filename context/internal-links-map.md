# Nexter WP Internal Links Map

When writing blog posts or content, naturally link to relevant pages below using the suggested anchor texts. Always prefer deep product pages over the homepage. Prioritize linking to pages that match the article's topic.

---

## Linking Rules

1. **Every blog post needs 3–5 internal links minimum**
2. **Always link to the most specific page** — e.g. link to `/nexter-blocks/blocks/wordpress-countdown-timer/` not just `/nexter-blocks/`
3. **Vary anchor text** — don't use the same phrase twice in one article
4. **Link to the pricing page in almost every post** — once, placed naturally
5. **Audience pages (`/nexter-for-*`) work well in conclusion sections**
6. **Never link to external competitor URLs in content**
